## WHY THIS MATTERS

> Devon has some of the best agricultural land in England. The farms around Holsworthy have been producing food for centuries — the soil, the climate, and the farming traditions here are genuinely exceptional. The Ruby Red Devon cattle breed, the rich pasture, the South West's mild and wet climate — all of it produces food that you can taste the difference in.
>
> We're not going to pretend that buying local makes us a charity. It doesn't — we do it because the food is better, because our guests can taste the difference, and because we think the relationship between this pub and the farms around it is worth preserving.
>
> It also means that when you eat at the White Hart, your money stays in the area. That matters to us.

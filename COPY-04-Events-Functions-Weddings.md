### FAQ

> **Is there a room hire fee?**
> We have a small hire fee which may be waived depending on the catering spend. Get in touch and we'll work out what makes sense for your event.

> **Can I bring my own cake?**
> Yes — cakes from outside are absolutely fine. A small corkage charge applies if you bring your own wine.

> **Can you accommodate dietary requirements?**
> Yes, with advance notice we can accommodate most dietary needs. Please let us know at the enquiry stage.

> **Is there parking?**
> Yes, free on-site parking for your guests.

> **Can I decorate the space?**
> Yes — within reason. No open flames, no glitter (the kitchen team will never forgive us), confetti cones outside are fine.

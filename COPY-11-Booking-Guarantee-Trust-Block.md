## DESIGN NOTES

**The Promise box (Component 1):**
- Use a light background distinct from the page (cream, warm off-white, or a very light green tint)
- Left border accent in the brand green (#2D4A3E) — draws the eye without dominating
- Not a pop-up. Not a modal. Always visible, never intrusive.

**Trust strip (Component 2):**
- Horizontal row of icons + labels
- Use consistent icon style throughout — line icons, not filled
- Works in footer, in sticky mobile bar, and as a band between content sections

**Review quotes (Component 5):**
- Real names, real quotes only
- Star rating displayed as actual stars, not text
- Link to Google Reviews so users can verify — this is a trust signal in itself
